Technology

Solving enzyme engineering as a search problem.

Aether is the first company capable of searching sequence space to find novel biocatalysts. Our technology can design enzymes for an ever increasing range of chemistries, all in timelines that fit your schedule.

Aether's robotic factory runs millions of experiments to index sequence space.

Commercially available automation falls short of the throughputs required to index sequence space.

Even recent improvements to automation fall short of the throughputs required to index sequence space.

Aether’s proprietary hardware allows massively parallelized experimentation, generating datasets orders of magnitude larger than the competition.

Aether’s algorithms search trillions of enzymes.

As Google mapped the internet, Aether maps trillions of enzymes to create a searchable index.
